Football

football is one of the must common games and popular games in world. football, also called assocaiation football or soccer, is a games involving two teams of 11 player who try to maneuver the ball into the other team's goal without using their hands or arms. the team that scores more goals win.

rule of football

football, game in which two teams of 11 player, using any part of their bodies except their hands and arms, try to maneuver the ball into the opposing team's goal. only the goalkeeper is permitted to handle the ball and may do so only within the penalty area surrounding the goal. the team that scores more goals win.
